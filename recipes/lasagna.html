Lasagna

Description

In this classic lasagna recipe, sheets of pasta are layered with a cheesy filling, a rich meaty tomato sauce, and more cheese and then baked until bubbly and browned

Ingredients

    lasagna noodles
    cheese
    ground beef
    onion
    garlic
    pasta sauce

Steps

    heat oven and boil water for pasta
    cook beef, onions, garlic in a skillet
    stir in pasta sauce and spices to ground beef mixture
    when pasta is ready, begin layering noodles and meat sauce with cheese until the pan is filled
    cover with foil and bake for 45min
    remove foil and add more cheese. Bake uncovered until cheese is browned and bubbling
